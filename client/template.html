Welcome

Welcome to SematAcc, the Essence Accelerator system!

This tool is designed to speed up the learning of Essence Theory in Software Engineering (see Semat website) and to easily test it with any software project in your company.

The tool is implemented by S4FS at the Free University of Bozen-Bolzano in Italy and it is free for everyone to use.

We are seeking for Essence enthusiasts to join in to the development of the SematAcc.
If you became interested, please contact professor Pekka Abrahamsson at firstname.lastname(at)unibz.it.

See the About page of this website for more information.
